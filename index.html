PORTFOLIO

Here you'll find a list of projects I've worked on. I've included a brief description of the project, the tools I used, and a link to the code.

Cyclistic Bike-Share

For this project, I used R to analyze a bike-share company's data to determine how to best convert casual riders into annual members. I used R to clean the data, create visualizations, and perform statistical analysis.

Bellabeat

Analyzed fitness data to help the company improve its marketing strategy and increase the number of sales. This highlights my ability to analyze data and make recommendations based on the analysis.

Vehicle Price Prediction - Part 1

Developed a machine learning model that will predict the price of a vehicle. As a result, the model and the conclusions drawn from the data can be used to inform the decision-making process of a company, or a buyer.

Vehicle Price Prediction - Part 2

This is the second part of the vehicle price prediction project. In this part, several machine learning models were built to predict the price of a vehicle. The models were evaluated using mean absolute error and root mean squared error. The best model was then used to predict the price of a vehicle.

NLP With Hotel Review Part 1

For this project, I used Python to analyze hotel reviews to determine the most common words used in the reviews. I used the Natural Language Toolkit (NLTK) to clean the data and perform the analysis.

NLP With Hotel Review Part 2

This is the second part of the hotel review project. In this part, I used machine learning models to predict the rating of a hotel based on the review. The models were evaluated using a confusion matrix and accuracy score. The best model was then used to predict the rating of a hotel.
